Participants need to book their accommodation themselves. We recommend the following hotels:

  • Hotel Zollamt (There are rooms pre-booked until September 30.)
  • Stadthotel
  • Art Hotel Lauterbach
  • Hotel Bremerhof
  • Alcatraz - Hotel am Japanischen Garten
  • Best Western Hotel Kaiserslautern
  • Hotel Hampton by Hilton Kaiserslautern
  • B&B Hotel (There are rooms pre-booked until September 30.)
