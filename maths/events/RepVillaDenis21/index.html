ABOUT

The meeting happens on invitation only and is limited to 15 participants.

FINANCIAL SUPPORT

    • HYGIENE AND INFECTION PREVENTION

    • The meeting happens on invitation only and is limited to 15 participants (according to the current Corona-Bekämpfungsverordnung of the State of Rhineland-Palatinate).

    • Testing: all particiants of the workshop are required to make a rapid covid-19 antigen test within 24 hours of the start of the meeting. A test centre is located on the campus of the TU Kaiserslautern, another one in Enkenbach-Alsenborn for last-minute tests.

    • Accommodation: participants will be accommodated in the guest house of the Conference Centre in order to avoid contact with external persons.

    • Contact to staff members: the conference centre is organised so that contact to staff members is almost reduced to zero.
