13.07: Reaching the Villa Denis


On Thursday, 13.07.2017, the meeting takes place in the Conference Center Villa Denis of the TU Kaiserslautern, located in the hamlet of Diemerstein in the Ortsgemeinde Frankenstein.

To reach the Villa Denis by train or by car, one needs to travel to Frankenstein (Pfalz). Then from the station in Frankenstein, it's a 5-minute walk up the road to Diemerstein.

  • From KL: it's a 10-minute ride on the S-Bahn from Kaiserslautern Hauptbahnhof to Frankenstein (Pfalz).

  • From Karlsruhe: Take an RE Train to Neustadt(Weinstr)Hbf and then either an S1 Train or an S2 Train to Frankenstein (Pfalz).


  • From Mannheim: Take either an S1 Train or an S2 Train to Frankenstein(Pfalz).


Tip: when booking train tickets on the Deutsche Bahn website, uncheck the box "schnelle Verbindungen bevorzugen". This will give you more connections to Frankenstein.
